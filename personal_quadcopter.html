Personal Quadcopter

This is the quadcopter I built during the summer of 2014. It is a 250mm size quad.


Specs:
Frame: "Spanky" 250mm Mini Quad Frame (G10 Fiberglass)
Motors: Sunnysky X2204S 2300kV Brushless Motors
ESCs: Drone Matters HK Blue Series 20A RapidESC
Flight Controller: Naze32 Flight Control Board
Controller/Transceiver: Turnigy 9X
Batteries: Zippy Compact - 1000mAh (35C), 1800mAh (35C)

Due to the flight control board being very popular at the time, I had to wait awhile for it to ship. In the meantime, I had all the other components and wanted to test it out. So I set it up running from an Arduino and spare IMU I had. The setup was a bit messy, but it was functional!

Designed and created by Aaron J. Kramer.
