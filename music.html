Music

I began playing music around the beginning of elementary school. I started out learning organ, and later moved to piano. In fourth grade I picked up the saxophone, and have played saxophone ever since. I also picked up the clarinet going into high school.

In elementary school I played in the Concert Band as well as the Jazz Band. I played the alto saxophone in both, as well as the bari saxophone for a year in jazz band. In middle school I played in the Concert Band, Wind Ensemble, Jazz Band, and Pit Band, playing alto saxophone for all of those. In high school, I played in the Symphonic Band, Wind Ensemble, Jazz Band, Marching Band, Pit Band, and District Band. During those I played both the alto saxophone as well as the clarinet. I also got a soprano saxophone in my sophomore year, and formed a sax quartet with three of my friends. We practiced and arranged to play short pieces at some of the band concerts.

Now currently attending college at the University of Maryland, I play saxophone in the University Band. I hope to possibly play in the Jazz Band here as well.
